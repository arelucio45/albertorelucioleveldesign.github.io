Senior Level Designer

Senior Level Designer with 7+ years in the games industry. Currently at Alkimia Interactive, where I spent six years contributing to Gothic 1 Remake alongside the LD team — from early blockouts through to ship.

Specialised in open-world RPG layout, blockout-to-final pipelines, and LD documentation. Degree in Interactive Product Design with a focus on game design — final thesis on level design in Resident Evil, awarded with Distinction.

Started in QA, moved through game design into level design, with early experience across mobile titles before transitioning to AAA.
